LEOPARD
DETAILS

Height: 18”-26” | 46-66 cm
 Length: 27”-51” | 69-130 cm
 Weight: 25-120 lb | 11-54 kg
 Tail Length: 24”-39” | 61-99 cm
 Lifespan: 17-22 years

APPEARANCE

The Leopard is a stunning and exotic breed. Its coat is a masterpiece of nature, adorned with intricate rosettes and spots that vary from rich golden hues to a frosty grey, mimicking the leopard's camouflage in the wild. Its fur is velvety and soft, making it a pleasure to touch. The eyes of the Leopard are striking, usually a mesmerizing shade of emerald green or amber, giving it an intense and alluring gaze. Its lithe and athletic body is built for agility, reflecting its wild ancestry, and it moves with a graceful and fluid motion.

TEMPERAMENT

The Leopard possesses a unique blend of wild curiosity and domestic charm. This breed is highly independent. This breed is highly intelligent, curious, and playful, often engaging in interactive games and exploring its surroundings with a sense of adventure. The Leopard is known for its confident and assertive personality, exuding a quiet strength and elegance. It enjoys perching on high vantage points, observing its environment with a watchful eye.

CARE + MAINTENANCE

The Leopard requires a stimulating environment that encourages its natural instincts to climb and explore. Providing a variety of toys, scratching posts, and puzzle feeders will keep this intelligent breed mentally and physically engaged. Regular grooming is necessary to maintain its beautiful coat, and occasional interactive play sessions are essential to bond with this active and playful feline companion.

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS

Due to its wild lineage, the Leopard Cat needs a responsible and experienced owner. Proper socialization and positive reinforcement training are crucial from an early age to ensure it becomes a well-adjusted and well-behaved pet. It's important to provide ample opportunities for exercise and mental stimulation to prevent boredom, as a stimulated Leopard is a content and happy one.
