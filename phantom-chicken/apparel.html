Fabric content is something that can affect your shirt choices.
- Cotton (100% cotton)
- CVC (60% Polyester + 40% Cotton)
- Tri-Blend (50% Polyester + 25% Cotton + 25% Rayon)

Fabric weight is listed in ounces.
The smaller the number, the thinner the fabric.
3.4 oz. = tissue weight
5 oz. = basic t-shirt
8-12 oz. = fleece.

We will need graphics or artwork to print your shirts.

Preferred art files would be created in a vector format with no placed images, and with all fonts turned to outlines.

This can be sent as an .ai, .eps, or .pdf file.

Everything here is printed by hand on a manual press. Ink is mixed and pushed across a screen using a squeegee (and arm strength).

The ink goes through the screen in open areas, onto a T-shirt. The ink is then heated to set it into the fibers of the shirt.
